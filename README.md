Last Updated: 14 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user information when you use the mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect limited information required to provide its core functionality.

Location Data

The App may request access to your device location to calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical location.

Location data:
	•	Is processed locally on the device or temporarily used to retrieve prayer times.
	•	Is not stored on our servers.

Users can disable location access at any time through their device settings.

⸻

Device Information

The App may automatically collect certain device information to improve functionality and performance, including:
	•	Device type
	•	Operating system version
	•	App version
	•	Language preferences

This information is used only for technical diagnostics and app performance improvements.

⸻

Notification Preferences

If users enable notifications, the App may store notification preferences locally on the device to send reminders for prayer times or daily content.

No personal identifiers are stored for this purpose.
